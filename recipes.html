Forget hours spent baking. Use this recipe to create mouthwatering cakes with the magic of the microwave.

Double Chocolate Protein Mug Cake

Ingredients

  • Chocolate protein powder, 2 tbsp
  • Coconut flour, 1 tbsp
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder, 1 tbsp plus 1 tsp
  • Pure organic stevia blend, 1 tbsp plus 1 tsp
  • Baking powder, 1/4 tsp
  • 100% pure pumpkin, 3 tbsp
  • Egg white, 2 tbsp
  • Vanilla extract, 1/8 tsp

Directions:

  • Lightly spray a large coffee mug with cooking spray, and set aside.
  • Mix cake ingredients together in a bowl to make batter.
  • Spoon batter into mug, then microwave 45 seconds to 1 minute.
  • Dump out your cake on a plate to cool.
  • Mix up chocolate sauce and pour over cake, plus dark chocolate chunks if desired. Enjoy!
